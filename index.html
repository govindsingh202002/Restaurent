About
We Leave A Delicious Memory For You
The Restau-rent a unique vegetarian restaurant in India was founded in 5 November 2022 . According to the owner, dining itself is an art which offers immense scope of experiments and innovations. That,s the reason why flavours are mingling magic resulting into satiating masterpieces. Our idea was to strike an interesting balance between our culinary delights and an exotic decor to complement with. The Restau-rent is a veg family restaurant which sets an uber-cool rustic ambience resembling the bygone era of the human evolution. The entire decor captures the enthralling feel of the Stone Age dining and offers a unique experience to be relished forever.
